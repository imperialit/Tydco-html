Mobile beauty services… inspired by experience

Hello, we’re Mohammed and Chris, and we believe in beauty treatments on your terms.

That’s the ethos behind Tydco, which stands for To Your Door Co.

Our business was born of our own frustrations, when Mohammed was forced to queue at a local barber shop for two hours for a haircut because it didn’t accept bookings. He found it difficult to find another barber who could cut his type of hair, so each experience left him frustrated, either sacrificing several hours of time or the quality of the service he received.

We thought there had to be a better way, so we decided to create one.

Mohammed's mother worked as a hairdresser for 20 years and owned a salon for 10 years, so we had inside information on the industry and what it was like for professionals as well. We discussed ways they could increase convenience for both professionals and clients, and the Tydco platform for mobile hair and makeup was born.

A change for the beauty industry

Hair and beauty professionals help us adapt our looks to the latest styles, so we thought it's time these industries received an update as well. By brainstorming ways to disrupt the usual industry standards, we came up with an innovative idea, a platform that makes waiting rooms a thing of the past. Beauty and hair are getting a makeover of their own, with a 'to-your-door' service designed to increase convenience for:

  • People who prefer in-home hair and beauty treatments at home
  • People who aren't able to visit a barber or a beauty salon
  • People who want more cost-effective options
  • People looking for local assistance with short booking times
  • People looking for mobile beauticians for luxury functions or events
  • People who can't do their own makeup or hair

The way we live our lives is different and more immediate. We want things when they are convenient to us. So why shouldn’t that be the case with beauty and grooming? And from our experience so far, our Pros love Tydco too.

Tydco's Mission

To disrupt the beauty and grooming industries, to change and simplify the traditional standards. We're creating a new type of convenience, revolutionising the way consumers access their self-care needs.
